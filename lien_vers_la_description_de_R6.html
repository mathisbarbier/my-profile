Rainbow Six Siege

Description

Rainbow Six Siege is a tactical shooter game developed by Ubisoft. It emphasizes team combat and strategy, where players can choose from a variety of operators with unique skills to accomplish objectives.

Gameplay

The game unfolds in rounds where one team attacks while the other defends. Players must use tactical destruction, strategic positioning, and coordination to succeed.

Operators

Operators are characters with special abilities. They are divided into different special forces units from around the world, each with their own gadgets and weapons.

Game Modes

Rainbow Six Siege offers several game modes, including Multiplayer mode, Cooperative mode, and seasonal events.
